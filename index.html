Common questions

How do I connect with my partner?
From your Profile, send an invite to your partner's email or share your unique connection code. Once they accept, you'll be linked together.
What happens to my daily photo after midnight?
Daily photos disappear at midnight so the app stays focused on the present — but anything you save to your shared Timeline stays put.
Is my data private?
Yes. Content is shared only between you and your connected partner. Features like cycle tracking are private by default and only shared if you choose to. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete my account or data?
Email us at justus.support@gmail.com with your account email and we'll process your deletion request promptly.
